# Privacy Policy for Idle Empire Tycoon

**Last Updated: 2026-03-01**

This Privacy Policy describes how **Idle Empire Tycoon** ("the Game", "we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use our mobile game.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

**Idle Empire Tycoon does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information.**

The only data the Game stores is local save data (game progress such as money, business levels, upgrades purchased, and achievements unlocked) written to your device's local storage using Unity's **PlayerPrefs** system. This data:

- Never leaves your device
- Is never sent to any server
- Is not accessible by us or any third party

We do **not** collect:

- Your name, email address, or any contact information
- Device identifiers or advertising IDs (unless ads are enabled — see Section 3)
- Location data
- Usage analytics or crash reports
- Any other personal information

---

## 2. How We Use Information

Since we do not collect personal information, we have no personal data to use. Local save data is used solely to preserve your game progress between sessions.

---

## 3. Advertising (AdMob)

If the Game includes advertisements powered by **Google AdMob**, Google may collect certain device information to serve relevant ads. This includes, but may not be limited to, advertising identifiers and device information. Google's data practices are governed by the [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).

You may opt out of personalized advertising on your device through your device's privacy or advertising settings.

If the current version of the Game does not include advertisements, this section does not apply.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

Other than Google AdMob (if applicable, as described above), the Game does not integrate any third-party SDKs, analytics services, social media platforms, or other external services that collect data.

---

## 5. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

The Game is intended for a general audience, including users under the age of 13. **We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 years of age.**

Because the Game does not collect any personal information, it is designed to be safe for players of all ages. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately using the information in Section 7.

---

## 6. Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device. We do not transmit any data, so there is no risk of a data breach on our end. You are responsible for the security of your own device.
